our famous dishes

Amygdalota

Amygdalota is gluten-free almond cookie that's widely popular in greece and are the perfect match for a warm cup of greek coffee, wether it is familly or friends gathering Amygdalota reflects good relationships, this food may varry a little throughout the different regions in greece but almonds are the main component

courgette balls (Kolokythokeftedes)

you are going to add that veggie start up to the list of your favorite dishes.this is a very popular cretan meze. Your taste buds will be mesmerized by its flavoured texture, salty and tasty feta cheese with abit of fresh mint. the original Kolokythokeftedes must be crispy on the outside with a creamy texture inside.

Dolmadakia

this is another sign of the versatile nature of greek food. Dolmadakia are tiny dolmades that are made of stuffed grape leaves. inside the leaf you will get meat of ground lamb or beef (sometimes both) and rice stuffing. Traditionally this plate is served as appetizer with lemon wedges

Baklava

Baklava is the quintessential greek food experience. this cuisine mostly contains nuats and butter along eith sugar. After baking sweet syrup is pured over so that the syrup can be absorbed by the crispy layers of the phyllo. It brings a festive mood in the dining room through its expensive flavour and flaky crust.
